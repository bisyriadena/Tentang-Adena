Quite Hopes

These are the hopes I carry quietly—things I pray for, grow into, and leave to time. Nothing grand, just a life that feels warm, enough, and sincere.

I hope to have and build a loving and peaceful family of mine, where there is understanding, support, and warmth even during difficult times.

I I hope to repay my parents’ sacrifices, not only through success, but by being present, caring, and making them proud.

"Tapi Diterima"

As a quiet closing, I chose a song that feels close to my heart. Tapi Diterima — Nadin Amizah


“Belum pernah aku, berusaha sebegitu, tapi di tanganmu aku tumbuh.”


These lyrics mean that with someone’s care, support, and acceptance, we can grow and transform in ways we never imagined.

I hope to become financially stable and comfortable, enough to live without constant worry and to enjoy life in a simple way.

I hope to have friendships that last, growing together and staying connected even as life changes.

I hope to complete my studies until the end, staying consistent and strong.

I hope to continue greeting my classmates in the future, or at least sharing a smile—keeping things kind and respectful even after time passes.

I hope to do my best in my deen—as someone who strives in faith, actions, and intentions. For any shortcomings, I seek Allah SWT’s guidance and forgiveness with an open heart.

I hope I’m remembered gently—as someone who smiled often, kept trying even when it was hard, and meant well. For any mistakes along the way, I seek forgiveness with an open heart.
