My Dearest: Artist

Olivia Dean Olivia Dean’s music feels warm and comforting. Her voice has a gentle way of slowing everything down, and I ESPECIALLY love “It Isn’t Perfect but It Might Be” and “Let Alone The One You Love” for their honesty and the reminder that it is okay to be imperfect while still striving to become better.
SZA SZA became my top artist on Spotify, and her music holds a special place for me. I especially love SOS Deluxe: LANA, as the album feels honest, emotional, and deeply relatable, making it one of the projects I always return to.
Tenxi This might sound unexpected, but as time goes by, I started to like Tenxi’s music. Once you get into the hipdut vibe, it’s genuinely enjoyable.
Ariana Grande Her music feels honest and emotionally layered, capturing themes of love, growth, and self-reflection in a way that feels real rather than forced, making her songs timeless and easy to return to.
Nadin Amizah I love Nadin Amizah because ALL of her music feels intimate and poetic, almost like reading a personal journal. Her lyrics are gentle yet deep, often expressing emotions that are hard to put into words, which makes her songs comforting and relatable. Listening to her music feels like being understood quietly, without needing too much explanation.
Bernadya I love Bernadya because her music carries a quiet sadness that feels honest and unforced. I’ve been lucky to see her live in concert twice, and even in those lively moments, there was a bittersweet depth to her songs that stayed with me. Listening to her feels like being allowed to sit with my feelings — sad, reflective, but somehow comforting.

For me, no thriller movie can top the Fear Street trilogy, and Part 2: 1978 is hands-down the best. I love how the story is told in reverse—it keeps you in constant suspense. It’s that 'Aha!' moment when you realize 'Oh, so this is why she is like that' or 'This is where the curse began' that makes it so satisfying to watch. My Dearest: Little Joy

Walking & Nature I enjoy briskwalking or taking walks with my earphones on, soaking in nature’s beauty. I used to love hiking and even tried marathon running, but due to my asthma, I have to avoid heavy activities. Simple walks keep me refreshed and happy.
Sightseeing & Sunsets I enjoy sightseeing slowly, relaxing in calm places, and watching sunsets, taking time to truly appreciate each moment.
Food Exploration I enjoy exploring food, trying new desserts, street food, cooking simple meals, and sharing meals with friends.
Respect & Timeliness I appreciate punctual people, as timeliness shows respect and thoughtfulness toward others.
Meaningful Moments I treasure meaningful social moments, like late-night talks, comfortable silences, and sitting quietly with people where we don’t have to force conversation.
Netflix times ! I like watching K-dramas, romance movies, comfort shows, especially watch new movies at the cinema and sharing horror movies with friends.
Concerts and Emotions I enjoy attending concerts and music events, as they let me feel fully connected to music and emotions.
Makeup and Confidence I love doing makeup at a relaxed pace, experimenting with soft looks, and creating photo dumps.
Music and Playlists I love listening to music, creating playlists based on my mood, and finding comfort in songs and lyrics.
